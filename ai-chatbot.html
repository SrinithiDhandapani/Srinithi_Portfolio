AI Chatbot for Mental Health

Supporting rural students with accessible mental health guidance

Overview

An AI-powered chatbot designed to provide instant, confidential emotional guidance and mental health support for rural students. The solution addresses the gap in mental health resources in underserved areas by delivering 24/7 support through easy-to-use mobile platforms.

Built with empathy and accessibility in mind, the chatbot offers stress management techniques, basic mental health awareness, and a safe space for users to express their feelings without judgment.

Key Features

  • Instant, confidential emotional guidance
  • Stress management techniques and exercises
  • Basic mental health awareness content
  • Mobile-first design for rural accessibility
  • AI-driven conversational responses
  • Easy-to-use interface for all age groups
